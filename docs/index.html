Country Total Emissions Visualizations

The following interactive maps are created on total emissions data, and transformations of this variable, from 2000 to 2022. Countries whose year of max emissions occurred from 2017-2022 are omitted. If the target variable has a significant negative slope, its emissions are declining. A significant positive slope indicates the emissions are rising. The Min-Max Normalization is as follows: (x - min(x)) / (max(x) - min(x)). This has been used to more easily compare between countries regardless of absolute emissions.
